Sell physical items with NFC & PayPal

GapTap allows you to add NFC tags to items you want to sell at a Yard sales, car boot, Makers Faire or whatever and then when someone taps on the item it gives them details about the item and offers them the ability to buy it right there and then with PayPal.

When something is purchased you'll instantly receive notification!

  • For Android 4.0+
  • Supports NFCA, NFCB, NTAG 203, Ultralight and others
  • Makes use of the new PayPal android libraries
